'Truth is a friend' - by Amelie Bee Brunyee

    One rainy day,
    lightning brightens your eyes,
    full of tears of truth:
    as the words spill out of your mouth,
    like that perfect hot chocolate tipping on the floor,
    with its warm scent.
    
    The words are bullets,
    piercing through where all my
    love, intentions and dreams lay.
    
    The truth is a blanket on a cold day,
    it keeps you going through harsh times.
    Without truth your life is a lie;
    you feel exposed
    for the world to see love, intentions, dreams and the truth
    that were built up inside you like building blocks.
    
    The truth is a weapon,
    the truth is a friend,
    the truth is your life from beginning to end.
